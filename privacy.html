Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how Expo971, we collect, use, and protect the personal information of its participants, sponsors, and partners.

Data Collection

Expo971 may collect personal information through registration forms, emails, event applications, sponsorship agreements, surveys, and direct communication.
This information may include:

  • Full name

  • Payment or transaction details (where applicable)

Use of Information

The personal information collected is used for:

  • Managing participation, booth allocation, and sponsorship agreements
  • Communicating event updates, schedules, and logistics
  • Providing marketing or promotional material related to Expo971 or its cultural initiatives
  • Ensuring safety, compliance, and coordination

Payments and Security

Expo971 does not store credit card or sensitive financial information. All payments are processed securely through authorised payment platforms. We take reasonable technical and administrative measures to safeguard your information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

Cookies and Website Tracking

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience, analyse site traffic, and provide relevant content. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though some website features may not function properly.

Consent

By submitting your information to participate or sponsor Expo971, you consent to the collection and use of your data as described in this policy. You may withdraw your consent or request updates to your data at any time by contacting the Expo971 team.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of receiving promotional communications

Requests can be made by contacting us at info@expo971.ae or through the contact form on our official website.

Introduction

Expo971 is a celebration of Emirati heritage, craftsmanship, and culture. It brings generations together to preserve traditions, strengthen identity, and inspire the future.

After the success of Expo971 in Dubai 2024, this year’s edition will be held in Ras Al Khaimah from 1–7 December 2025, offering visitors an authentic experience of Ras Al Khaimah’s cultural identity.

Program Highlights

  • Daily heritage and cultural shows
  • Folklore, poetry, and artistic evenings
  • Workshops in traditional crafts like Sadu, Talli, pottery, and palm frond weaving
  • An artisan market featuring handmade products and perfumes
  • Traditional food and Arabic coffee experiences
  • Storytelling sessions and children’s activities
  • Heritage fashion shows and competitions

Participants

Expo971 invites artisans, schools, cultural institutions, and creative individuals to take part in this year’s celebration of Emirati heritage and craftsmanship in Ras Al Khaimah (1–7 December 2025).

Participants will have the chance to display their work, connect with local and international audiences, and contribute to preserving the UAE’s living traditions.
